# Conclusions and Outlook Templates

This folder contains templates and guidelines for the **Conclusions and Outlook** chapter of a PhD thesis built around conference-style core chapters in empirical CS/SE. This chapter is thesis-specific: it closes the research questions, synthesizes findings across chapters, and looks forward to implications, limitations, and future work.

## Chapter overview

The Conclusions and Outlook chapter has three main roles:

- **Close the loop on the research questions** by revisiting them explicitly and stating what the thesis has answered.  
- **Synthesize findings across core chapters**, so the reader sees an integrated picture rather than isolated studies.  
- **Look outward** to implications, limitations, and future directions, and end with clear concluding remarks.

It is not a repeat of individual chapter summaries; instead, it operates at the thesis level, showing how the pieces fit together and what they mean.

## How to use these templates

Suggested workflow for candidates:

1. Draft the **core chapters** first using the paper-style templates.  
2. Once the main studies are stable, use this chapter to:
   - Explicitly answer each research question across chapters.  
   - Synthesize what the thesis as a whole has found.  
   - State implications, limitations, and future directions at the thesis level.
3. Use `concluding_remarks.md` to write a compact closing that feels like a natural endpoint for the document, not just a summary.

The aim is that, by the time this chapter is written, the thesis reads as a **coherent research program** rather than a collection of disconnected papers.
